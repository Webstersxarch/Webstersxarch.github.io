THE PROBLEM

Every bill you forget to log is a number your books don't know about.

01 — LOST PAPER

Vendor receipts pile up on a truck dashboard or a jacket pocket, and half never make it to a spreadsheet.

02 — MANUAL ENTRY

Typing vendor, amount, and date into a sheet by hand at the end of a long day is easy to skip.

03 — MIXED FORMATS

One vendor texts a photo, another emails a PDF invoice. Neither fits a single simple process.

04 — STALE BOOKS

Bills recorded weeks late mean your budget numbers are already out of date by the time you see them.

HOW IT WORKS

Every bill runs through the same four checkpoints, automatically.

CHECKPOINT 01

Capture

A Gmail label catches vendor bills the moment they land — no forwarding, no digging through the inbox.

CHECKPOINT 02

Sort

The attachment's file type decides the path: a photo goes to an AI vision model, a PDF goes through text extraction.

CHECKPOINT 03

Extract

The AI reads the vendor name, amount, and date off the bill and puts them into a consistent structured record, with a fallback if a field is missing or unclear.

CHECKPOINT 04

Record

A new row lands in your Google Sheets ledger automatically — the bill is logged before you've closed the email.
