THE PROBLEM

Every unanswered message is a job that goes to someone else.

01 — TIMING

Messages come in while you're on a roof or under a sink. By the time you reply, the homeowner already messaged three other contractors.

02 — REPETITION

Most replies ask the same five questions every time: size, scope, timeline, budget. That's time you're not spending on the actual job.

03 — TIRE-KICKERS

Not every message turns into work. Sorting serious leads from "just curious" still eats up your evening.

04 — SLOW QUOTES

A ballpark number sent back fast wins more jobs than a perfect quote sent two days late.

HOW IT WORKS

Four stages, running automatically, every time someone messages the page.

A

Capture

A message hits the Facebook Page. The automation catches it the instant it arrives — no checking the inbox required.

B

Qualify

An AI Agent asks the same questions you'd ask: project type, size, timeline — in a normal conversation, not a form.

C

Price

The answers get matched against a live pricing sheet, so the estimate reflects your actual rates, not a guess.

D

Hand off

A real estimate goes back to the homeowner, and serious leads get flagged for you to follow up personally.
